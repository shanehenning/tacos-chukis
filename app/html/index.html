A TACO LOVE STORY

SEPARATED BY 1,700 MILES

A broke student, recently graduated from the University of Washington and with no job prospects to speak of, bought a bicycle and rode it 1,700 miles down the coast back to his hometown of Tijuana.

His family greeted him with the tacos from his childhood that he had so dearly missed--and could find nowhere in Seattle.

And the dream of Tacos Chukis was born.
