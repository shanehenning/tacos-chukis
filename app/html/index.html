A TACO LOVE STORY, SEPARATED BY 1,700 MILES.


A broke student, recently graduated from UW and with no job prospects to speak of, bought a bicycle and rode it 1,700 miles down the coast to visit his hometown of Tijuana.

His family greeted him with the tacos from his childhood that he had so dearly missed--and could find nowhere in Seattle.

And the dream of Tacos Chukis was born.

AUTHENTIC MEXICAN CANTINA STYLE FOOD

From rotisserie-roasted adobada pork to grilled prickly pear cactus leaves, with a generous garnish of guacamole and maybe topped with a chunk of grilled pineapple, Tacos Chukis offers authentic Mexican cantina fare.
